## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Electric Griddles For Breakfast Cooking

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations

The first step in selecting the best electric griddle is to define your cooking scenarios. Ask yourself: How many people do I cook for? A small surface (around 100 square inches) suffices for one or two people, but larger families or frequent entertainers should look for at least 200 square inches. Consider the types of dishes you'll prepare. Breakfast foods like pancakes and French toast require ample space to avoid crowding, while eggs and bacon can be cooked in batches. If you enjoy cooking beyond breakfast, a griddle with a broad temperature range (200-420°F) can handle everything from warming tortillas to searing steaks. Some models include warming trays or lids for added versatility. Additionally, think about your kitchen layout. Do you have enough counter space to
