## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Infrared Heater Wall Mount

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Matching the heater to your specific environment is crucial. For outdoor or damp spaces like patios or garages, prioritize an IP rating of IPX4 or higher to ensure weather resistance. Indoor-only models may lack moisture protection but can offer quieter operation. Consider mounting flexibility: some units only wall-mount, while others include ceiling or freestanding options. Also, assess the square footage you need to heat. A 1500W infrared heater typically covers up to 200 sq ft effectively, but larger spaces may require multiple units or higher wattage.
